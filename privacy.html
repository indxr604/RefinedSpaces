Privacy Policy

At Refined Spaces, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you interact with our website or services, such as:

  • Your name, email address, and contact information when you fill out forms or request services.
  • Payment details when you make a purchase or book a service (securely processed by third-party payment providers).
  • Other information you voluntarily provide, such as feedback, reviews, and inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide, improve, and personalize our services. Specifically, we may use your information for:

  • Processing your orders, requests, and inquiries.
  • Improving our website and services based on your feedback.
  • Sending you promotional materials and updates (you can opt-out at any time).
  • Ensuring the security of our services and preventing fraud.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. These measures include:

  • Secure encryption protocols for data transmission.
  • Access controls to limit unauthorized access to personal information.
  • Regular security audits and updates to ensure the protection of our systems.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with third-party service providers who help us run our website or deliver services to you, such as:

  • Payment processors to handle financial transactions securely.
  • Service providers who assist with marketing, website maintenance, and analytics.

We ensure that these third-party providers respect your privacy and use your information solely to provide the contracted services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our website or at the email address provided below.

If you wish to opt-out of receiving promotional emails, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our emails.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve website functionality, analyze website usage, and provide personalized content. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may impact your ability to use certain features of our website.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at:

Email: refinedspaces65.com
